$$
|A_1 \cup A_2 \cup \dots \cup A_n| = \sum_{i=1}^{n} |A_i| - \sum_{1 \leq i < j \leq n} |A_i \cap A_j| + \sum_{1 \leq i < j < k \leq n} |A_i \cap A_j \cap A_k| - \dots + (-1)^{n+1} |A_1 \cap A_2 \cap \dots \cap A_n|
$$
For 2 sets, for example
$$|A∪B| = |A| + |B| − |A∩B|$$
